Hello! I'm a math PhD student at the Université de Bretagne Occidentale. I work under the supervision of Professor [Johannes Huisman](http://huisman.perso.math.cnrs.fr/). My dissertation is scheduled for January 2024. I'm also on the job market this year. Last year I worked as a Temporary Assistant Profesor (ATER in France) at the Université d'Angers and I'm currently a Teaching Fellow again in Angers. Here's a copy of my [CV](https://storoo.github.io/files/CV.pdf)     

I'm interested in (equivariant) homotopy theory and its interations with algebraic geometry. Throughout my PhD, I delved into a simplicial approach to $$C_2$$-isovariant homotopy theory, with potential applications in real algebraic geometry that are yet to be explored. I'm also interested in higher category theory. Currently I work in a project that explores constructions of model category structures whose fibrant objects capture the notion of Grothendieck 2-fibrations.
